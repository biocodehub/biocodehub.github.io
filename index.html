Abstract

We're using modern programming languages like Python and Julia to explore interesting topics in biology. By taking advantage of community resources and packages available for these languages, we're currently focusing on areas like biological simulations, cell image analysis, and genome sequencing analysis. However, our research isn't limited to these fields and will expand further as new needs and interests arise.
